RiboBase is the name for the collection of all ribosomal data in our database, and the resources related to other databases. It will include a Wiki aiming to be a comphrehensive resource of other ribosomal databases. A long term goal would be developing an interface between the popular ribosomal databses so that their data can be analyzed and visualized with our tools.

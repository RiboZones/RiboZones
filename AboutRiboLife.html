RiboLife is a collection of sequence alignments for ribosomal rRNA. Currently there is one version for the LSU provided.

RiboZones Alignment

Version 1.0 of the RiboZones alignment has been released in paper in 2014. You can download it here.

The alignment contains 135 sequences with broad phylogenetic representation from each domain of life (Bacteria, Archaea, Eukarya). The 23-28S rRNA sequences in RiboZones are full-length, aligned according to secondary and tertiary structure, and contain a single entry per species.

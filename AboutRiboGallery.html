RiboGallery is a collection of ribosomal rRNA secondary structures, containing precomputed data. It contains ready to use high-resolution png images. It also contains SVG files, which is the best supported and simplest vector-graphics format. SVG files can be easily ediited either through text, or through programs such as Adobe Illustrator, Canvas, and InkScape.

RiboGallery is located at http://apollo.chemistry.gatech.edu/RibosomeGallery/index.html.
